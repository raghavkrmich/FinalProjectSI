Global Music: Music is international and is enjoyed by nearly every person around the world. The different genres in English Music are also applicable for music across the world. Some of the styles that this website explores are Indian, Latin, and French Music. Visit the Global Page to tour music from across the world!
Rap Music: Rap Music is one of the most popular genres among teenages and young adults today. From getting its start in the 1970s as street level music to now a commercially thriving genre it has branched off into many different sub-genres such as Emo Rap and Pop Rap. Explore the Rap Page to tour the different types or rap music and even enjoy some sound bites!
Rock is a combination of many different types of music. The start of Rock N’ Roll can be heard in songs from the 1920s, and the genre still continues to even today’s music. Combining jazz, blues, gospel, country, and many other genres, rock appeals to people of all ages and all walks of life.
